# **App Name**: Divine.Co

## Core Features:

- Product & Category Management: Full CRUD operations for products, including support for multiple variants (color, quantity, images), and dynamic category management with unique slug validation.
- Order & Inventory Sync System: Admin interface for creating/editing invoices, tracking order statuses (e.g., Pending, Accepted, Delivered, Cancelled), and automatic deduction/restoration of stock based on order changes.
- AI-Powered Marketing Copy Tool: An integrated AI tool to assist administrators in generating concise and engaging product descriptions or marketing taglines based on product attributes and keywords.
- Dynamic Storefront Catalog: Public-facing display of products, categorized dynamically from Firestore, with rich product cards showing price variations, optional 'Hot Sale' tags, and a real-time search popover.
- Persistent Cart & Secure Checkout: User shopping cart with variant selection (using Zustand for persistence), a streamlined checkout process featuring shipping options, coupon integration, and Cash on Delivery.
- Core Analytics & Sales Overview: Admin dashboard presenting key performance indicators like total sales, daily sales via a bar chart, total profit, and current stock value to provide immediate business insights.
- External Integration Suite: Implementation of Facebook Pixel and CAPI for tracking 'ViewContent', 'AddToCart', and 'Purchase' events, alongside floating WhatsApp and Messenger buttons for customer support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated rose (#C86B73), selected for its elegant and warm yet restrained presence, perfect for evoking a premium feminine boutique feel.
- Background color: A soft cream (#FFF1D6) that provides a luxurious, inviting canvas for the content, contrasting beautifully with the primary and accent colors while maintaining an overall gentle aesthetic.
- Accent color: A delicate muted pink (#E89AA3), analogous to the primary, used for highlighting interactive elements or key information without overpowering the design, ensuring an elegant and harmonious balance.
- Headline font: 'Playfair Display' (serif). Body font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif). Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Elegant, thin line-art icons that complement the clean aesthetic of ShadCN UI, maintaining a feminine and sophisticated feel throughout the application.
- Spacious and clean layout emphasizing product imagery and readability. Fully responsive design, optimized for mobile-first browsing and streamlined administration across various devices.
- Subtle, smooth animations and transitions, particularly for hero banners, product interactions, and dashboard data visualization, enhancing the user experience without being distracting.
